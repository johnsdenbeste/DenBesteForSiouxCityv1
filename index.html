Meet John

John brings a diverse background in finance, public service, and civic leadership. A graduate of Morningside University with a degree in Political Science, he works as a financial advisor helping individuals and families with tailored advice and solutions to achieve their financial goals and dreams. John plans to bring the same skills and passion to city budgeting.

John proudly serves his community in law enforcement as a Reserve Deputy with the Woodbury County Sheriff’s Office. He is an active member of the Siouxland Chamber of Commerce Government Relations Committee, including the past two years as chair, where he has been a strong voice for Sioux City at the state and federal levels.
